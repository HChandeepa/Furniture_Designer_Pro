Our Story

Who We Are

Founded in 2010, Furniture Designer Pro began as a small workshop with a big vision: to revolutionize the way people design and experience furniture. What started as a passion project between three design school graduates has grown into a leading furniture design platform serving customers worldwide.

Our team of expert designers, craftsmen, and technologists work together to create furniture that blends timeless craftsmanship with cutting-edge design technology. We believe that everyone deserves beautiful, functional spaces that reflect their unique personality and needs.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 50,000 customers create their dream spaces, with showrooms in 12 cities across 3 continents.

Our Design Philosophy

Where Craft Meets Innovation

At Furniture Designer Pro, we bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern technology. Each piece in our collection begins with hand-drawn sketches by our master designers, then comes to life through a combination of artisanal techniques and precision engineering.

We obsess over every detail - from the sustainably sourced materials to the ergonomics of each design. Our proprietary design platform allows customers to customize every aspect of their furniture while ensuring structural integrity and design coherence.

Whether you're looking for a statement piece or a complete room solution, our designs are created to stand the test of time both in style and durability.
