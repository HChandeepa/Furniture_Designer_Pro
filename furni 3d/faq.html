Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Ordering is simple! Browse our collection, select your items, and add them to your cart. When you're ready, proceed to checkout where you'll enter your shipping information and payment details. You'll receive an order confirmation email once your purchase is complete.

For custom designs using our Designer Tool, you'll need to finalize your design before adding it to your cart.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days within the continental US. For custom or made-to-order items, please allow 2-3 weeks for production before shipping. You'll receive tracking information as soon as your order ships.

Expedited shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Duties and taxes may apply depending on your country's import regulations.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. The item must be in its original condition, unused, and in the original packaging. Custom or made-to-order items are final sale and cannot be returned.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number. You'll be responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error.

How does the Designer Tool work?

Our Designer Tool allows you to create custom furniture pieces tailored to your exact specifications. Simply select a base design, then customize dimensions, materials, colors, and finishes. The tool provides real-time pricing and 3D previews of your design.

Our system ensures all custom designs meet our structural and quality standards. Once finalized, your custom piece will be handcrafted by our artisans.

What materials do you use in your furniture?

We use only premium, sustainable materials including:

  • Solid hardwoods (oak, walnut, maple)
  • Eco-friendly engineered wood
  • High-quality upholstery fabrics
  • Low-VOC finishes and adhesives

Each product page details the specific materials used in that item.

How do I care for my furniture?

Care instructions vary by material:

  • Wood: Dust regularly with a soft cloth. Use a wood-specific cleaner occasionally.
  • Upholstery: Vacuum regularly and treat stains immediately with appropriate cleaner.
  • Metal: Wipe with a damp cloth and dry thoroughly.

Detailed care instructions are included with every purchase.

Do you offer assembly services?

Yes! We offer white-glove delivery and assembly service for an additional fee. Our delivery team will bring your furniture into your home, assemble it, place it in your desired location, and remove all packaging materials.

Standard delivery includes curbside drop-off only. Assembly instructions are included with all items that require assembly.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

We can often modify or cancel orders if contacted within 1 hour of purchase. For custom items, changes may not be possible once production has begun. Please contact our customer service team immediately if you need to modify or cancel your order.

Still have questions?

Our customer service team is happy to help with any additional questions you may have.
